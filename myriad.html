what is sento arena?




Sento Arena is an online 2D multiplayer fighting game with a diverse storyline and 12 different playable characters. Each character possesing their own unique abilities and skillsets that players can learn and master.



Follow Nyaa on his conquest in training all-over the world whilst encountering different foes and overcoming many difficult battles all in order to become: "The Sento Champion"



Join in an amazing online experience where you can compete and battle against other players and friends. Engage in online-multiplayer combat ranging from casual brawls to intense competitive tournaments with everything on the line. Play in a competitive ladder placing you in a different rank dependant on your skill; Bronze, Silve, Gold, Platinum, Diamond and Champion. Download Sento Arena today to see if you have what it takes to become "The Sento Champion!"
