what is sento arena?




Sento Arena is an online 2D multiplayer fighting game with a diverse storyline and 12 different playable characters. Each character possesing their own unique abilities and skillsets that players can learn and master.


Follow Nyaa on his conquest in training all-over the world whilst encountering different foes and overcoming many difficult battles all in order to become: "The Sento Champion"


An online experience where you compete and battle against other players. Engage in online-multiplayer combat ranging from casual brawls to intense competitive tournaments with everything on the line. Play in a competitive ladder where you can have different ranks dependant on your skill; Wood (0-499), bronze (500-999), silver (1000-1499), gold (1500-1999), platinum (2000-2499), diamond (2500-2999) and champion (3000+). Join the battle today and become the Sento Champion.
